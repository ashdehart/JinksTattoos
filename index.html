Meet the Artist

Jolie Lowder, also known professionally as Jinks and recognized online as Instagram @j.Lnks, is a rising tattoo artist whose work blends intentional linework, thoughtful design, and a distinct artistic voice. This website serves as both a gallery and a booking space, where clients can explore her portfolio, purchase original art prints, and schedule tattoo appointments directly.

Jolie is currently tattooing at Garden of Ink, a woman-owned tattoo studio in Ogden, Utah that prioritizes creativity, inclusivity, and craftsmanship. Alongside other experienced artists, she continues to refine her technical skill while developing a style that is expressive, detail-driven, and deeply personal.

Her work spans custom tattoos, flash designs, and standalone illustrations, with a focus on clean execution and meaningful collaboration. Whether you are looking for a custom piece, a carefully curated flash tattoo, or a high-quality print to collect, Jolie approaches every project with care, professionalism, and respect for the client experience. Jolie is currently accepting new clients.

Before You Book

Studio Policies

Tattooing is strictly limited to clients who are 18 years of age or older. Valid, government-issued identification is required at every appointment.

A non-refundable $50 deposit is required to confirm and secure your appointment. Your deposit will be applied toward the final cost of your tattoo. Design work will not begin until a deposit has been received. Jolie offers free touchups for a year after the initial tattoo is completed.

Your deposit will be honored and transferred if you reschedule at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled appointment. Rescheduling with less than 24 hours' notice will result in forfeiture of your deposit. If you do not arrive within 20 minutes of your scheduled appointment time without prior communication, your appointment will be cancelled and your deposit forfeited.

All pricing below are for custom designs. Flash is priced separately. Session times reflect active tattoo time, not the total appointment length. If the time limit is reached, there is a 30-minute window to wrap up — anything beyond that requires booking a new session. A 2% processing fee applies to Venmo and card payments. If the tattoo takes less time than the amount slotted, the full price still stands unless otherwise stated by the artist. Payment is due in full at the end of your session.

  • Short session — Up to 2.5 hours · $110/hr · $100 minimum
  • Half Day session — 3 hours · $300 flat
  • Full Day session — 6 hours · $600 flat

Please disclose any relevant medical conditions, allergies, or medications prior to your appointment. This includes but is not limited to: blood disorders or use of blood-thinning medications; skin conditions, allergies, or sensitivities; any disorders or diseases that could impact the process of being tattooed or healing a tattoo.

Jolie does not copy artwork or designs created by other artists without their explicit, written consent. Jolie takes artistic integrity seriously and asks that you do the same. Jolie does not use AI-generated imagery in their design process, and she will not tattoo any design that has been created using AI tools. All work is original and hand-crafted.
